Java Lab - ASCIIArt

Assignment:

For this project you will modify the ASCIIArt.java program.

Change the ASCIIArt.java program so that it prints your first name. However, your name should not be printed normally, but in block letters; i.e., if your name is George, the modified program should print something like

  


 GGGG
G    G
G        eee    ooo   r rr    ggg    eee
G       e   e  o   o  r   r  g   g  e   e
G  GGG  eeeee  o   o  r      g   g  eeeee
G    G  e      o   o  r      g   g  e
 GGGG    eee    ooo   r       gggg   eee
                                 g
                              g  g
                               gg

This would be printed as ten different lines, the first of which is " GGGG", the second is " G G" and so on. You may make your block letters as simple or fancy as you like. Creating images using ASCII characters is known as ASCII art. Some examples of ASCII art can be found on the web at http://www.afn.org/~afn39695/collect.htm. a famous example of ASCII Art animation can be found at http://www.asciimation.co.nz/.

Instructions:

  1. Write the program, compile, run, and verify its screen output

  2. Include your name as a documentation statement and a brief description of your program.

  3. Submit your program to Web-CAT.
